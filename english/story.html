Michelle had a meeting so she woke up a bit earlier than usual, got ready and checked the program that she left running the night before. She asked him the reason to be so elegant and Jonathan, a bit nervous, answered that he had been looking for the PERFECT moment to tell her something, but that after thinking about it for a long time he reached the conclusion that actually every moment together was always perfect and that he wanted all of his life like that. Finally, a little voice inside her head told her to stand up, while Jonathan was kneeling, showing the most beautiful ring with the infinite sign and saying in perfect Spanish: "Silvia Michelle Torres Pacheco, would you accept this engagement ring and be my wife?"
